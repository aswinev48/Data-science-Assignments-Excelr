## üìò About This Assignment

The primary objective of this notebook is to apply rigorous statistical tests to determine if observed patterns are statistically significant or occurred by chance. Key tasks include:

* **Hypothesis Testing:** Formulating Null ($H_0$) and Alternative ($H_1$) hypotheses for various business and scientific scenarios.
* **Test Selection:** Applying the correct statistical tests such as **Z-tests**, **T-tests**, **ANOVA**, and **Chi-Square** tests.
* **P-Value Analysis:** Interpreting p-values against significance levels (e.g., $\alpha = 0.05$) to reject or fail to reject the null hypothesis.
* **Confidence Intervals:** Calculating intervals to estimate the true population mean or proportion with a specific degree of certainty (e.g., 95%).
* **Error Analysis:** Understanding Type I and Type II errors in the context of statistical decision-making.
